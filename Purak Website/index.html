The mission of the APCYS is to promote an atmosphere of scientific research and innovation among students in secondary schools (junior and senior high schools) in the Asia Pacific region through research completions. The previous APCYSs were organized in Palanga Raya (Indonesia), Palembang (Indonesia), Taipei (Taiwan), Kuala Lumpur (Malaysia) and New Delhi (India). The 6th edition of the conference will be hosted in Nepal by Tribhuvan University, Research Centre for Applied Science and Technology (RECAST) and jointly organized by Nepal Polymer Institute and Brainycube Research Organization in collaboration with different Universities and professional institutions. Nepal, with her unparalleled cultural and biodiversity, look forwards to welcoming the young scientists from all over the world in the lap of Mt. Everest.
